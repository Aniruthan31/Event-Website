RAJALAKSHMI ENGINEERING COLLEGE

Rajalakshmi Engineering College, an autonomous institution affiliated to Anna University, Chennai, was established in the year 1997 under the aegis of Rajalakshmi Educational Trust whose members have had consummate experience in the fields of education and industry.

SOCIETY OF MECHANICAL ENGINEERS

Society of Mechanical Engineers is a society that focuses upon Engineering and Technology. It was established in the year 2002-2003, since then the society has taken many progressive steps in imparting practical knowledge. The Society excels in many specialisations other than the specialisation in the mechanical field.

INGENIOUS

Krida Yanthrik is a National level Intercollege techno-sport fest conducted by Society of Mechanical Engineers at Rajalakshmi engineering college in order to bring different students from different departments under one roof! This year krida Yanthrik 2k17 is conducted at 25th September in our college premises in a better way!
