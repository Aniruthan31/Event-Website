TREASURE HUNT

Are you a treasure hunt enthusiast, here is the platform. Unleash the jack sparrow in you!!

Instruction:

  • 5 teams will be playing per round
  • Each team must contain a maximum of 3 players
  • The clue script for the first hunt will be given at the starting point
  • Each round consists of 5 hunts
  • Teams will be collecting the parts of a puzzle in each hunt
  • After completing 5 hunts each team will be having enough parts to assemble a component
  • Only simple parts will be given along with the assembly guide

  • Teams will complete each hunts by locating the latitude and longitude using their smartphone
  • The team which completes 5 hunts + assembly of the parts at the least time will be considered as the winner of that round
Winners will be selected based on all round winners and the team with the least time will be announced as winners

Rules:

  • Each team must contain atleast one smart phone with any kind of latitude and longitude locating app available in the app store
  • Team members should not interact with any one out of the team till you completely finish the event , if found the team will be disqualified immediately
  • If you found other team’s clue by any chance never tamper it. A violation of this rule could cause disqualification of your team.
